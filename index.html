objectCSS jQuery plugin

objectCSS allows you to expose the HTML structure of your OOCSS.

Features:

  • Quickly visualize where a particular object is being used.
  • Expose sub-classes that are extending your objects.
  • Expose the deeper structure of your objects.

Example 1:

Highlight your '.nav' objects:

$(".nav").objectCSS({depth: 0});
